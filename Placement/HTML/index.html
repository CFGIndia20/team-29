What is the Issue?

In India, over 180 million youth will enter the workforce in the next 15 years, most of whom are poor, school dropouts or unemployable. we have to significantly increase our investment in them to open the doors of capabilities and opportunities. our response mechanisms prevalent today to bridging this skill deficit is far behind on both quality and scale. to design for this requires building a strong sectoral infrastructure, long-term commitment and impact focus. The/Nudge Centre for Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (CSDE) is India’s first institution dedicated to creating a thriving skilling ecosystem that is able to deliver on the employment aspirations of India’s youth and set them on a path of life-long learning.

What we do?

Nudge was founded on the belief that every human being deserves a life of dignity out of poverty. with a purpose to alleviate poverty, sustainably and scalably, we started in 2015 as a collective of leaders, entrepreneurs and change-makers from corporate, government and development sectors, with an enduring commitment to solve India’s greatest human development challenge. since then, we have evolved in our journey as a poverty alleviation organization by expanding our levers of change from skill development for youth to social entrepreneurship to rural development.
